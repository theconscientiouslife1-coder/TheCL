This blog is to provide a guide to assist people who feel overwhelmed by the modern world, who want to take control of their lives and make a difference while having a sense of balance as to who one truly is and being at one with their environment/world/externus.

This is a non-denominational site (there is no belief system implied or being referenced), and what is written is from a humanist perspective. It is hoped that what is written can provide a semblance of hope and allow one to utilise what is conveyed and can be applied to one's everyday dealings in the most positive and proactive manner possible.

In the age of Individualism, technology plays on the fears and preferences which further isolates us. This blog aims to assist people in lifting the veil (veil of maya) that restricts those from attaining their true self and see how that perpetuates into society.

When we seek to change aspects of our life, our nature is to think of the whole change and with even the greatest will this leads us to being overwhelmed. The key to substantial change is incremental change. This blog will unite all the aspects of person's life and how to attain a balance and a sense of worth.

These themes are divided between the self and the external, which shows how interconnected we all are.
